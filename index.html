Research and Development (R&D)

R&D is the major driving force for Panama Seeds Pvt.Ltd. Pvt.Ltd., through innovative approaches in developing products suitable for varied climates and requirements. Panama Seeds Pvt.Ltd. Pvt.Ltd. senior & young talented R&D team has competent plant breeders, plant pathologists, foundation seed, and parent seed administration teams separately. Panama Seeds Pvt.Ltd. Pvt.Ltd. high quality R&D facilities at Hyderabad, Guntur, and Nellore are spread over 12 hectares and well-equipped pathology laboratory.

Panama’s R&D Goals

• To develop innovative and desired hybrids and varieties for the farmers of the country on priority.
• To achieve higher yields, superior quality, disease resistance, heat tolerance and better nutritional quality of vegetable seeds to cater the demand for the growing population (No:1 populated country on the earth).

Marketing and Sales (M&S)

Panama’s M&S supports its five hundred and more channel partners by creating farmer demand for its seeds by various means like field activities, testimonial advertisements, print media and social media to reach the end users. The optimistic impact of Panama on farmer yields, profits, and improved living standards helps the product demand.

Processing and Packaging

Panama Seeds Pvt.Ltd. Pvt.Ltd. process, treat and packs its seeds using modern equipment in an appropriate environment. As an aggressive and fast level growing organization we are using different methods like online sales, through our distributors and C&F agents to reach our valued end customers in the country.

Production

The first step involved in the production of our high quality seeds is the identification of suitable locations by our Production Team. Panama Seeds Pvt.Ltd. Pvt.Ltd. production team procedures and practices ensure best quality seeds in quantities as per the government policies for meeting domestic customer requirements. Locally assigned managers monitor the plots closely over the duration of the crop. They give technical guidance on crop management and seed production techniques right from sowing to harvest of the crop. At the Quality Lab the seeds are tested for genetic and physical purity, moisture and germination. All tests carried out are in accordance with ISTA standards. Only the seed lots that fulfill the entire criterion are then consigned to the Packing Unit.
